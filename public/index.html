How to Play

Overview

A social deduction game where Liberals try to stop the Fascists from gaining power.

Teams & Roles

  • Liberals: Enact 5 Liberal policies or eliminate Hitler.
  • Fascists: Enact 6 Fascist policies or elect Hitler as Chancellor.
  • Hitler: Must avoid suspicion and survive.

Game Flow

  1. Election: President nominates a Chancellor.
  2. Voting: All players vote on the government.
  3. Legislative: President and Chancellor enact a policy.
  4. Executive: President may gain special powers.
