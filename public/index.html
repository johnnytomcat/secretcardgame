How to Play

Overview

A social deduction game set in a Victorian mansion. House Guests work together to maintain order, while the disgruntled Home Staff secretly conspire to put their Butler in charge.

Teams & Roles

Role Distribution (4-6 players):

  • 4 players: 2 House Guests, 1 Home Staff, 1 Butler
  • 5 players: 3 House Guests, 1 Home Staff, 1 Butler
  • 6 players: 4 House Guests, 1 Home Staff, 1 Butler

Team Knowledge:

  • House Guests: Don't know anyone's role
  • Home Staff: Know who the Butler is
  • The Butler: Doesn't know who the Staff members are

How to Win

House Guests Win If:

  • 5 Guest policies are enacted, OR
  • The Butler is exposed and dismissed

Home Staff Win If:

  • 6 Staff policies are enacted, OR
  • The Butler is appointed Head of Household after 3+ Staff policies are enacted

Game Flow

  1. Election: The President nominates a Chancellor candidate.
  2. Voting: All players vote AYE (Yes) or NAY (No). Majority wins.
  3. Legislative Session: If the vote passes:
    • President draws 3 policies, passes 2 to Chancellor
    • Chancellor enacts 1 policy
  4. Executive Action: The President may gain special powers (see below).
  5. Next Round: Presidency passes to the next player.

Executive Powers

When Staff policies are enacted, the President gains powers:

  • 3rd Staff Policy: Examine top 3 cards in the deck
  • 4th Staff Policy: Dismiss a player
  • 5th Staff Policy: Dismiss a player

Dismissal Rules:

  • The President must choose a player to dismiss from the mansion
  • Dismissed players are eliminated and cannot vote or hold office
  • If the Butler is dismissed, House Guests win immediately!
  • The dismissed player's role is revealed to all

Special Rules

  • Term Limits: The previous President and Chancellor cannot be nominated as Chancellor
  • Butler Danger Zone: After 3 Staff policies, appointing the Butler as Chancellor wins the game for the Home Staff
  • Election Tracker: If 3 governments fail in a row, the top policy is automatically enacted (chaos!)
  • Deck Reshuffle: When fewer than 3 cards remain, the deck is reshuffled

Strategy Tips

  • House Guests: Pay attention to voting patterns and policy claims. Trust no one completely!
  • Home Staff: Blend in with the Guests. Create confusion and distrust.
  • The Butler: Act like a Guest. Your team wins if you become Chancellor late game.
